ABOUT US

Annaporna Organic Industries & Annaporna Seeds was established in the year 1980.We are the business of manufacturing of micronutrients and other customized nutritional products for plants. We started our activity by manufacturing and distributing a small range of mineral feed additives for plants and other agricultural use.We manufacture a variety of Fertilizers,Pesticides,Insecticides and Seeds. We mainly deals in Urea Guard,Neem coated compounds,Annaporna Gold and other kinds of Nitrogeneous compounds. We often conduct research on compounds that could deliver mineral nutrients to plants more efficiently.We are a major distributor for:Aries Agro ltd,Micro Plex Chemicals and Fertilizers,Nandi Agro ltd,Bio Seeds..etc.
